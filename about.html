About Us

Judges That Fights For Your Freedom

PNK Legal LLP is a full-service law firm based in India, with a distinguished practice spanning for about forty decades. The firm specializes in Service and Employment Law, Constitutional Law, Labour Laws, and Dispute Resolution.

Over the years, PNK Legal LLP has represented a diverse clientele ranging from large multinational corporations to employees’ service unions and individual professionals. We combine in-depth legal knowledge with extensive practical experience to deliver strategic, effective, and result-oriented legal solutions tailored to each client’s needs.
